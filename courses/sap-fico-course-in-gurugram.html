SAP ERP Course Syllabus

Our comprehensive curriculum is split into logical modules to guide you from fundamentals to advanced skills.

  • Main Topic 1: Enterprise Structure & Chart of Accounts — Company Code, Fiscal Year Variant (FYV), Posting Period Variant (PPV), Chart of Accounts (COA)
  • Main Topic 2: General Ledger Accounting (FI-GL) — Master Data, Document Types, Number Ranges, Posting Journal Entries (`FB50`/`F-02`)
  • Practical Capstone: Enterprise SAP Financial Organization Setup & GL Posting Workflow
  • Main Topic 1: Accounts Payable & Automatic Payment (APP) — Vendor Master Data, Invoice Posting (`FB60`), APP Run (`F110`), Down Payments
  • Main Topic 2: Accounts Receivable & Asset Accounting (FI-AA) — Customer Master Data (`FB70`), Dunning Run (`F150`), Asset Classes, Depreciation Run (`AFAB`)
  • Practical Capstone: End-to-End Vendor P2P & Customer O2C Financial Cycle Execution
  • Main Topic 1: Cost & Profit Center Accounting — Controlling Area, Primary/Secondary Cost Elements, Cost Center Hierarchy (`KS01`), Profit Centers (`KM01`)
  • Main Topic 2: Financial Closing & Balance Sheet Reporting — Month-End & Year-End Closing, Financial Statement Versions (FSV), Balance Sheet / P&L Generation
  • Practical Capstone: Generating Financial Balance Sheet Reports & Internal Cost Allocations
